Welcome to the home page ! 

Here is a complete guidance for you to better understand how the system works

Profile

The profile page displays all personal information about an individual. It is a comprehensive and informative space that serves as a sort of digital resume. Here applicants can view their username, system-generated unique ID, age, gender and date of birth

User Management

Our school also offer various other means of communication, such as online portals or mobile applications. These tools can be particularly useful for parents and students who may need to access information or communicate with the school outside of regular office hours.

Program Management

School submission page is to facilitate the submission of enrollment or registration documents, which supply application forms and programme list. This can streamline the admissions process and ensure that all necessary information is collected in a timely and organized manner.
